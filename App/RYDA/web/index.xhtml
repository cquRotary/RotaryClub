Rotary Youth Driving Awareness

Young people aged 17-25 represent less than 15% of the population
yet account for over one quarter of deaths and injuries on our roads.
In 2000, a group of concerned Rotarians decided that something
must be done to stop this tragic loss of young people and
established the Rotary Youth Driver Awareness (RYDA) program, a
road safety education program for year 11 students.
